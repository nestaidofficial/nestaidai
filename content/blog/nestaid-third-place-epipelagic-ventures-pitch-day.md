We burned two of our pitch minutes just explaining what home health care even is.

Still walked off with **3rd place and $5,000**. First pitch of my life.

Nestaid placed at **Epipelagic Ventures Pitch Day** in Kendall Square on July 16.

## Pitching home care to a tech room

Here's the thing about pitching home care to a tech room: nobody's thought about the 6am call-out. A caregiver cancels, a client is waiting, and someone's grandmother might spend the morning alone.

I had to build that world before I could pitch in it.

## We showed one workflow

The one that wrecks an agency owner's week, every week: [the caregiver call-out](/blog/handling-caregiver-callouts-with-ai). A shift opens, the phones come out, and a coordinator's morning disappears.

We showed how Nestaid's AI agents run that entire playbook — spot the gap, call qualified caregivers in parallel, fill the shift, update the schedule. One workflow, start to finish. The room got it.

## The best part wasn't the check

It was the room — founders, sharp judges, people staying late just to talk. I learned more in one evening than in weeks of prep.

Congrats to **AttorneyAlert** (grand prize) and **Lending Automator** (2nd place). And thank you to **Epipelagic Ventures** for the stage.

## Where the $5K goes

Straight into token maxing. Nestaid's agents have expensive taste. 🤖

First pitch done. More to come.
